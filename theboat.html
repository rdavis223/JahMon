The Boat


Jah Mon is a Fractional Rig J29 with an Inboard Yanmar Single Cylinder Disel Engine. The J29 was designed by Rod Johnstone and built by J Boats Inc. The class was first introduced in 1982. The J29 is known for being a great racing/crusing vessel due to its fast design, comfortable cabin, and its ability to handle in rough conditions. Jah Mon was built in 1982 and was the third J29 class sailboat ever built (out of 298 hulls). Jah Mon previously went by the names "Minimum Payment" and "Winter Project" under previous owners. Jah Mon has a PHRF rating of 120, which is different from many J29 class sailboats because of its oversized spinnaker and inboard engine.
